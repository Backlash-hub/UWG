Black and Blue Steak Pittsburgh Style

by Andrew Green

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 strip loin steaks, (about 10 to 14 ounces) each
  • Salt
  • Freshly ground blak pepper

Directions

In a large cast-iron skillet, melt the butter. Season the steaks with salt and peper. When the butter is very hot and starting to brown, add the steaks. Cook the steaks for 3 to 4 minutes on each side. You want to almost char the outside of the steak and have the center cold. Remove the steaks from the pan and serve.
