Pancakes Recipe

by William Pevytoe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Bisquick pancake mix
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 tbs butter or Pam non stick spray

Directions

Combine the Bisquick, 2 eggs, and milk together in a bowl and mix well. Heat a pan on the stove to medium heat. Utilize the butter or Pam non stick spray on the hot pan. Pour desired amount of pancake mix onto the pan (recommend 1/3 cup). Flip once golden brown and you are ready to serve.
